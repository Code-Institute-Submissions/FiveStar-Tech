Hi, I'm Stuart Anthony, a qualified Personal Trainer / Combat Coach and owner of FiveStar Techniques.

FiveStar Techniques (also known as FiveStar Tech) is a bespoke personal training service, created to help its clients achieve optimum results. The core objectives are to work with and help clients become the best version of themselves, physically and mentally. Mentor and teach the importance of positive mindsets and becoming accountable - seeing your fitness journey as a lifestyle, not a chore.

With over 23 years of experience in Martial Arts, I have seen the benefits a functional discipline adds to a healthy lifestyle. With bespoke sessions incorporating a blend of combat sports, athletic protocols, yoga, or traditional compound exercises - while having fun at the same time.

Customer care, empathy, and raw passion are the drivers behind my efforts, helping people achieve their goals and maintain them as a way of life.
